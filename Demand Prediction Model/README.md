Gravity model is a model proposed by Newton to measure the force of attractiveness towards an object. However, this model has been used in macroeconomics and urban planning to estimate the potential demand of the new tube station based on spatial distribution of population and distance to the station. 

The model assumes that areas with larger population densities would generate more trips, while further distance from the station reduces the likelihood that people will use it. By combining these 2 factors, the model will produce a score for different coordinates and we will then normalise each value to be used for the parameter of our final multi scoring model.

In this project, the gravity model is used to generate a heatmap of potential demand across London.
